Midterm

Attempted to predict people's star ratings as accurate as possible based on their review without using any neural networks or deep learning methods. My process can be summed in two parts: feature engineering and model building. Used sentiment analysis tools like TextBlob and VADER, calculated basic text statistics such as length of setence and number of exclamation marks, and finally used TF-IDF and LSA on preprocessed text to hopefully capture any meaningful information from text columns (a brief summary and the actual review itself). Ultimately decided to use an XGBoost model because of its well-established performance in handling large datasets efficiently, robustness in capturing complex patterns, and versatility in classification (in this case, an ordinal scale of number of stars).
